NITS Lab

We are an academic research lab at the Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology, focusing on network systems, information security, artificial intelligence, and trustworthy computing.

Our mission is to build practical and reliable technologies for secure digital infrastructure, intelligent network operations, and privacy-preserving systems. We welcome students who are curious, disciplined, and excited about research with real-world impact.

Research

We develop methods and systems for reliable cybersecurity and network intelligence. Our work combines systems thinking, data-driven methods, and rigorous evaluation on practical scenarios.

-> Network & System Security:

  • Intrusion detection, traffic analysis, and threat intelligence
  • Web, cloud, and distributed system security

-> AI for Cybersecurity:

  • Machine learning for anomaly detection and malware analysis
  • Robust, explainable, and trustworthy security models

-> Privacy & Trustworthy Computing:

  • Privacy-preserving analytics and secure data sharing
  • Human-centered security and responsible AI systems
